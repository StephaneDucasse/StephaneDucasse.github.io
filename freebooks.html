Some years ago, I started to be fed up to see all the books I like to be out of print, so I started to contact authors and collected their old books. I would like to thanks them all and their publishers as well. If you
know an author that is willing to give to the community a book, please give him my email. You can also support me.

Recent Pharo books

You can find a lot more recent and free books (Spec, Pharo by Example Updated, Pharo with Style, Learning OOD with TDD, and many more) at http://books.pharo.org. Most the new books around Pharo are hosted at http://github.com/SquareBracketAssociates and each project has an automatic build with the latest PDF version.
